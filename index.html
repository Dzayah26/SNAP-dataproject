Make it SNAP(PY)!

Trends of SNAP recipients in each borough and who has the most.

By I. Darius Johnson

November 12, 2021

A local bodega in New York City where SNAP is accepted. Photo by I. Darius Johnson

Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program or SNAP is benefits that people use to purchase food at local grocery stores or bodega's. The program is in place for those who may need extra assistance in buying food for their household. Amounts given to those that qualify for the program can vary on how many are in your household and your yearly/monthly income. Some people that may rely on SNAP are disabled individuals, the elderly, and those with children

In New York City, many of those in need rely on SNAP to get the food they need that will get them through the month. However which borough that relies on them the most may come as a surprise to some. As of 2021 Brooklyn has the most SNAP recipients out of all the boroughs in New York City.
